A W4ll Out

A W4ll Out is a first person puzzle game, where you have to escape from a sarcastic AI by breaking the 4th wall... with a sledgehammer.

This game was made in 5 days for the GameGen Game Jam by a team of 5. It was made using Unity 2019.4.1f1. The themes of the Game Jam were Fourth Wall and loops.

Team members:

  • Marcos Agudo (Programming and director) - @mercadodev13
  • Fernando Barroso (Art and Sound) - @naxinix
  • Mario Márquez (Programming) - @mario_marquez98
  • Diego Sagredo (Programming) - @DiegoK_Dev
  • Pablo Sánchez (Programming) - @PabloSanchezDev

My part in this project was:

  • Design
    • Design the general game concept in collaboration with the rest of the team.
    • Designing 6 out of the 15 levels in the game.
  • Programming
    • Programming the walls basic behaviour (breaking animation and physics, changing numbers...).
    • Implementing 6 out of the 15 levels in the game.
    • Implementing player's interactions with the environment.
    • Created the rewind effect for when the timer hits 0 and the player gets moved back to the start of the game.

A W4ll Out turned out to be a great success in the context of the game jam, ranking #1 on the Overall category as well as on the Gameplay category. It also ranked #2 on Music & Sound and on Theme adaptability, #3 on Originality, and #8 on Art.
