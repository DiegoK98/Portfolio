Punch 'em Out

Punch 'em Out is a web game that makes you play as two giant fists on a bridge that have to prevent knights and wizards from crossing it. More and more enemies will keep coming as the waves go on, so let's see how many can you punch out to reach the high score in every level, but don't let too many of them through or you will lose.

This project was made by a team called Boops Games Studio: @boops_games

Team members:

  • Gabriel Muñoz (Art)
  • Carlos Ventura (Art, Design)
  • Diego Sagredo (Programming)
  • Mario Márquez (Programming)
  • Alejandro Hernández (Programming)

My part in this project was:

  • Design the general game concept in collaboration with the rest of the team.
  • Scripting the enemies behaviour.
  • Scripting the punches controller and player input.
  • Implementing the menu.
