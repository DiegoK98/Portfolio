Bot Clash

Bot Clash is a 4-player multiplayer game where you have to explore the environment and loot as many perks and weapons as you can. After this first phase of the game, you will have to fight to death in an arena. Last robot standing takes the victory!

This project was made by a team called Boops Games Studio: @boops_games
